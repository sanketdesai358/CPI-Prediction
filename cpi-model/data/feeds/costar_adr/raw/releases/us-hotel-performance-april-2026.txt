ARLINGTON, Va. – 9 June 2026 – U.S. hotel performance showed positive year-over-year comparisons, according to April 2026 data from CoStar, a leading global provider of online real estate marketplaces, information, and analytics in the property markets. 

April 2026 (percentage change from April 2025):

•    Occupancy: 64.9% (+1.6%)
•    Average daily rate (ADR): US$165.90 (+2.8%)
•    Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$107.73 (+4.4%)

Among the Top 25 Markets, St. Louis reported the highest increases in occupancy (+6.2% to 67.9%) and RevPAR (+13.8% to US$93.41).   

Miami registered the largest gain in ADR (+12.5% to US$283.34). 

Overall, 20 of the Top 25 Markets saw a jump in RevPAR.

About CoStar Group 
CoStar Group (NASDAQ: CSGP) is a global leader in commercial real estate information, analytics, online marketplaces, and 3D digital twin technology.
